Simple Pasta Recipe

ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt to taste

instructions

  1. Boil Water With Salt
  2. add pasta and cook for 10min
  3. drain and add oil

cooking instruction

step 1: prepare ingridients

chop all the vegies and measureout out your spices. make sure you have everything ready before you start cooking.

step 2: cooking pasta

bring water to a boil, add salt and cook pasta perrfectly according to package instructions.

step 3: make sause

in a separate pan, cook garllic in olive oil, then add tomatoes and simmer for 15min.

step 4: combine and serve

drain pasta, mix with sause, garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.
